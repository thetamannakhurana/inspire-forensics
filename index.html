Who We Are

Forensics Beyond
the Classroom

Inspire Forensics is the premier forensic science society at the National Forensic Sciences University, Delhi Campus. Founded in 2024, we are a pan-India community of passionate students dedicated to advancing forensic knowledge through practical application.

Our members span universities across India, from NFSU Delhi and Amity University to Galgotias, VGU Jaipur, Chandigarh University, and more, united by a shared passion for forensic science and justice.

From fingerprint analysis and crime scene investigation to toxicology, digital forensics, and forensic anthropology, our members engage across a wide spectrum of disciplines guided by expert faculty and industry professionals.

We have hosted 16+ expert guest lectures, 3+ inter-university collaborations, monthly newsletter publications (Traces and Trends), and comprehensive sample paper drives for NFAT and CUET aspirants across India.

"Our mission is to inspire the next generation of forensic scientists by creating opportunities that go beyond textbooks, through real investigations, expert guidance, and a community built on curiosity and justice."

Forensic Chemistry and Toxicology

HPLC method development, Trans-Resveratrol analysis in wine samples, chemical reagent testing, soil analysis, and biological sample examination.

Fingerprint Analysis

Latent fingerprint development using graphite powder dusting at light, medium, and high pressure levels with ridge detail comparison and classification.

Forensic Anthropology

Skeletal morphology analysis, bone classification, sex and age determination from human remains using osteological techniques in a dedicated lab.
